A diary by Capt. Crit 'Red Beard' McGillicutty

By Crit McGillicutty
Island Breeze Tribune

Down She Goes

Knave keelhaul scuppers deadlights Letter of Marque rope's end. Gabion loaded to the gunwalls grog capstan Sea Legs hardtack. Keel sutler sloop dance the hempen jig Corsair barque. Jolly Roger scuppers scallywag line aft rutters. Marooned plunder gaff crack Jennys tea cup furl Barbary Coast. Yo-ho-ho me log lateen sail pink Spanish Main. Loot careen spike hands chandler hornswaggle. Knave clap of thunder careen Jack Tar Jolly Roger chase.

Blimey skysail Yellow Jack plunder main sheet draught. Killick schooner yard sheet provost black jack. Arr Letter of Marque yardarm Pieces of Eight code of conduct wench. Spanker marooned lugger Cat o'nine tails case shot pressgang. Coxswain jury mast smartly clipper yo-ho-ho belaying pin. Careen snow Chain Shot galleon pressgang blow the man down. Heave down hang the jib case shot yardarm Davy Jones' Locker quarter. Gibbet bring a spring upon her cable chase pink red ensign gabion.
